Frequently asked questions

What is the pricing?

As I said in the portfolio section, the pricing is dependent on the client's needs and its difficulty. If you would like to pursue your project additionally with my help you should acknowledge that long time cooperation benefits in understanding each other better and both needs. Whether you are "just curious" of the developers prices do not waste both mine and your time.

What can be expected from my work?

Since polish fivem community mostly adores roleplay servers in this modification, then all of my works circulate around it. From coding from scratch various original job plugins to setting up custom models of building insides and appartments. My work may be divided into two sections: selling already done plugin packages or coding new from scratch. Of course if it is required I can also fulfill the role of setting up the server and mySQL for additional charges.

What payments do you accept?

Only paypal, no paysafecards or crypto.

What is the order of paying and receiving the plugin package?

After concluding the client's needs and price negotiations, the deadline of finishing the work is given. Halfway in work the client is mentioned of the progress and upon that, half of previously initiated price is required to be paid and upon receiving the money the work is continued. The other half is paid upon finishing the project before sending the files.
